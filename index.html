i HOW THIS CALCULATOR WORKS

Late Fee Rules

  • GSTR-3B / GSTR-1 Regular: ₹50/day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST), capped by turnover slab (₹2,000 / ₹5,000 / ₹10,000).
  • GSTR-3B / GSTR-1 NIL: ₹20/day (₹10 CGST + ₹10 SGST), capped at ₹500.
  • GSTR-9 (Annual): ₹200/day (₹100+₹100) tiered by AATO, capped at 0.04%–0.5% of turnover.

Interest Rule

Interest is charged at 18% per annum (or 24% for excess ITC/reduced liability cases) on the net cash tax liability, calculated daily from the day after the due date till the actual payment date.
